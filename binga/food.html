Food & Bar

Featuring seafood fresh from our own boats, homestyle cooking

with ingredients from neighboring farms, signature cocktails and more...

Check Cabin Availability

Fine Dining, Fast Food or a Feast?

Yes! Whether it's quick snacks, comfort food, low-cal food, vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, or a full night's barbecue extravaganza under the stars we've got you covered.

We're a bit remote, but we import in all the necessities to make impressive global fare. We have our own fishing boat so we have seafood well covered, but otherwise we do rely on local meats, produce and veggies - so we always mark what is currently available on the big chalkboard by the kitchen.

Please let us know any special needs and requests in advance so we can get it ready for you. Here our favorite dishes served below.
