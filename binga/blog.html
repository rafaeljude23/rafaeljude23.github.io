Palawan Travel Tips

The island and province of Palawan is regarded as the Philippines’ “Last Frontier” for a reason: many parts of it are remote, sparsely inhabited and abounding with unspoiled nature. That’s great and why we love it — but the downside is a lack of comprehensive and up-to-date travel info and advice. Not to worry, Binga Beach is here to fill you in! We’ve traveled Palawan extensively and share here insights from all our experiences.
