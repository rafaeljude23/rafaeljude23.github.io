Vendor Performance Management

With Vendor Performance Management, we assess your vendors' performance and find ways to optimise it. We evaluate their metrics and statistics, such as delivery times, prices, and quality control. This data-driven approach helps us evaluate vendor performance, with a particular focus on developing continuous improvement.

Vendor Performance Management is also a practice that helps businesses better manage their relationships with suppliers and vendors. This allows them to identify which are impeding business growth, bringing exceptional value, and where companies can benefit from the efficiency and cost savings through consolidation.

Not just for data

Measuring metrics and statistics means measuring the quality of their services and overall costs and measuring the actual success of the relationship with your vendors. This means looking at how responsive vendors are to your issues and measuring their ability to communicate with you effectively. We also take into account the buying organisation from vendors if applicable.

The identified businesses that achieve the highest rate of performance and reliability will be focused on to see where improvements can be made. Vendor performance management is crucial to any organisation's talent acquisition strategy. It helps to:

  • Inform you of decisions about the third-party relationship
  • Streamline administration
  • Identify the root cause of any issues you encounter
  • Utilise KPIs to hold vendors accountable and measure performance
