Our services include:

Vendor Performance Management

Vendor performance management is a key component of the vendor management lifecycle. It involves collecting and analysing data provided by vendors, which include financial, operational and technical metrics. This data helps to assess and optimise performance.

VMS Consulting

We are responsible for assessing the business requirements and goals, conceptualising a vendor management approach, identifying which processes can be reliably offloaded to vendors, providing individual vendor recommendations, and evaluating the impact of vendors on your business.

Learn More →

VM Systems

VM Systems enable businesses to streamline and simplify their vendor management processes. These systems offer a range of features, such as messaging, tracking, scheduling, invoicing and monitoring, enabling businesses to conduct all of their necessary day-to-day vendor management tasks and processes from one central solution.

Procurement Processing

When you have a business, procuring and managing your inventory can be time-consuming. Polarity has a complete, end-to-end solution for companies looking to reduce their in-house workload.
