Challenges

Air travel could cause many inconveniences and challenges to passengers due to the different moving parts involved in making travel seamless and smooth.

  • Passengers might have a hard time finding where their vehicle is parked after reaching their destination.
  • Travels could also struggle with handling their baggage, coupled with the lack of readily available baggage handlers.
  • Finding destination tours and guides is also another point of inconvenience for air travelers.
  • Finding the ideal shopping areas in unfamiliar placesis also not an easy task, adding to the list of challenges passengers experience during air travels.
